GenV is a South African consulting firm combining industrial psychology and data science to help businesses with talent recruitment, data analytics, and AI-driven decision-making.

GenV offers three core services: Talent (recruitment and technical hiring), Data Analytics (advanced analytics and modelling), and Consulting (customized solutions for client-specific needs).

GenV is led by Managing Director Vuyiswa Mataka, who holds a BCom in Industrial Psychology from the University of Pretoria, and Technical Director Gerald Bezuidenhout, who holds a BSc in Mathematical Statistics from the University of Pretoria.
